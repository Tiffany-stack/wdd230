Cebu Business Hub Chamber of Commerce

Site Purpose

The Cebu Business Hub Chamber of Commerce aims to:

  • Fuel Economic Growth: Act as a driving force for economic development in Cebu.
  • Catalyze Collaboration: Bring together local businesses for collaborative opportunities.
  • Virtual Community Hub: Provide a centralized online space for community interaction.
  • Support Innovation: Foster a supportive environment for local startups and innovations.

Scenarios

  • Business Networking: Discover upcoming events that facilitate business-to-business networking.
  • Contact Information: Easily find contact details for key figures in Cebu's business community.
  • Demographic Insights: Explore data on population growth, economic trends, and market insights in Cebu.

Color Schema

The color scheme reflects the dynamic and energetic business environment of Cebu. Primary Color: #1565c0 (used for headings and accents) Secondary Color: #4caf50 (used for paragraphs and background colors)

Typography

Font Selection: 'Poppins', sans-serif (used for headings and body text)
